CHAIN OF COMMAND

The Chain of Command is an official hierarchy of authority that dictates who is in charge of whom and of whom permission must be asked.

OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS

The Operating Instructions (OI) serves as a reference to cadets for any questions they may have (promotion criteria, chain of command, class procedures, demerits) and sets the guidelines for how AFJROTC should function as a whole.

KNOWLEDGE

Warrior Knowledge develops competent, ethical leaders by mastering Air Force fundamentals like the core values of Integrity First, Service Before Self, and Excellence in All We Do. Along with customs, history, and essential practical skills. This training builds character, sharpens critical thinking, and prepares cadets to lead and fight as officers in the Air Force and Space Force, ensuring mission success and upholding the highest military standards.
