RAIDERS

Raider is for those cadets who wish to challenge their physical endurance, mental strength, and their leadership. All cadets who participate in the Raider team will have a chance to strengthen their leadership, improve on their fitness, and participate in competitions against other school Raider teams. Raider Competitions are athletic competitions held at JROTC programs around the world. Events take place outdoors and usually consist of individual strength tests (push-ups, tire-drag, obstacle course, etc), distance team running events, first aid events, and some form of rope bridge construction/crossing.
