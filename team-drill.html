DRILL TEAM

The Drill Team is one of the two flights in the Drill and Ceremonies Squadron. Cadets must try out and be selected to join the Drill Team. Cadets here will focus solely on rifle movements, rifle spinning, and basic 30-step. Drill Team also requires physical acuteness as a degree of athleticism is required in order to perform all the proper movements.
